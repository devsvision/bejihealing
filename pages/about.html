About Beji Healing

A forest sanctuary designed for restoration and refined operations.

Beji Healing is a fictional premium retreat concept shaped for real hospitality workflows: program discovery, booking, payments, guest care, front office, finance, inventory, reporting, and settings. The interface uses native HTML, Tailwind CDN, and ES modules so it stays lightweight and backend-ready.
